Conquering the Galaxy 2

Description:

Conquering the Galaxy 2 is a 2D, Top-Down, Space Shooter game. The player fights their way through a series of levels and fights a variety of bosses. All levels can be played individually after they have been unlocked. However, beating multiple levels in a row is likely to get you a higher score.

Conquering the Galaxy 2 was developed as a personal project. The original Conquering the Galaxy was the first game I ever developed. Therefore, I thought it'd be fun to see how I would make the game again with more experience under my belt.

While all of the programming in this game has been done by myself, I did receive a lot of help from my friends. They provided a lot of assets including sprites, backgrounds, original music and even their voice. This project would not have turned out as it did without them.
