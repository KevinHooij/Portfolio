Function Dungeon

Description:

Function Dungeon is an educational game where the player has to find their way through a variety of dungeons while solving math problems to continue.

Function Dungeon was developed for The Science and Mathematics Education research group. They are researching how games can best be used to help pupils understand math in their education. I provided them with prototypes and concepts based on existing literature on how children learn and retain information. Combining this aspect with game design made for a fun and interesting challenge.

For Function Dungeon the concept and design were created by myself. I was also responsible for all of the programming. Assets were created partially by myself and by a third party.
